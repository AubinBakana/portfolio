Summary

In my quest to certify as a Microsoft Azure Solutions Architect Expert, I had already had the chance to work with MongoDB in some measures; however, the depth to which I was required to work with the platform did not give me the opportunity to delve deeper into this NoSQL database. Furthermore, I had already developed a taste for data engineering and architecting as evidenced by my other studies prior to this; and by the way, becoming an Azure Data and DevOp Expert is what I have been gravitating to, a next major milestone for me aside getting an ideal career opportunity. My research led to the discovery of MongoDB University, a platform that MongoDB Co. maintains to develop skills and expertise for the NoSQL database.

There is a fee for the Associate certificate, which I did not judge necessary to pay considering my limited budget at the time. However, I successfully completed all the courses and passed all tests in the learning path within a week, as detailed in the trancript below.

MongoDB Associate certificate is issued on successful completion of the "MongoDB Developer" learning path(which is the "Introductory to MongoDB" path plus 3 more modules in a language of choice) and related exam. I have assessed that my experience at Harvard puts me in an even more favourable position to complete remainder of this learning path within a day or 2. Certifications at MongoDB is subject to completion of course, hands-on labs and a multiple choice test based on the course and dedicated labs.

Couple the above with my experience in database in general, and newly gained computer science knowledge, I feel ever more confident in my ability to work with MongoDB. There's more, I intend to finish the learning path to become a MongoDB Expert in good time.
